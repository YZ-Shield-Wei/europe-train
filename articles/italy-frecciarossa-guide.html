Italy Frecciarossa High-Speed Train Guide

2026 Latest: italo Comparison, Fare System, Super Economy Early-Bird, Seat Classes

Updated June 2026 Reading time: 12 min

Key Takeaways

  • Frecciarossa reaches 300 km/h, connecting Italy's major cities
  • Super Economy early-bird from €9.9, opens 4 months ahead
  • italo is private high-speed rail, usually 20% cheaper than Trenitalia
  • CartaFRECCIA loyalty card can redeem free tickets
  • Rome Termini is the main hub — always confirm your departure station

4. Seat Classes

Trenitalia Seat Classes

  • Standard (Second Class): 2+2 layout, best value
  • Premium (First Class): 2+1 layout, more spacious, includes meal
  • Business: Individual seats, includes meal and lounge
  • Executive: Fully private, includes full meal and lounge
  • Quiet Zone: No phone calls

italo Seat Classes

  • Smart (Second Class): Basic seats
  • Prima (First Class): More spacious, includes meal
  • Club Executive: Individual seats, full service

5. Booking Tips

Savings Checklist

  • Buy Super Economy 4 months ahead (from €9.9)
  • Compare Trenitalia and italo prices (italo usually 20% cheaper)
  • Register for CartaFRECCIA loyalty card (earn points for free tickets)
  • Travel Tuesday to Thursday
  • Avoid August (Italian holidays) and public holidays
  • Use Trenitalia app (exclusive offers)
  • Consider italo Low Cost tickets (lowest prices)

6. Trenitalia App Guide

Download & Registration

  • iOS: App Store search "Trenitalia"
  • Android: Google Play search "Trenitalia"
  • Supports English interface (Settings → Language)

Key Features

  • Booking: Credit card, PayPal
  • Digital tickets: Mobile QR code entry
  • Real-time info: Delays, platform changes
  • Loyalty: CartaFRECCIA points program
  • Seat selection: Visual seat map

7. italo App Guide

Download & Registration

  • iOS: App Store search "italo"
  • Android: Google Play search "italo"
  • Supports English interface

Key Features

  • Booking: Credit card, PayPal
  • Digital tickets: Mobile QR code
  • Real-time info: Train status
  • Seat selection: Visual seat map
  • Promotions: Regular discount alerts

Important Tips

Italian high-speed rail is extremely popular; book 2–3 months ahead for peak season (July–August). Rome Termini is the main hub with 32 platforms — always confirm your platform number in advance.
