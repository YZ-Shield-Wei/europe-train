常见问题解答

如何预订欧洲火车票？

You can book through official railway websites (SNCF Connect, DB Navigator, Trenitalia), third-party platforms (Trainline, Omio), or at train stations. Online booking is recommended for early bird discounts.

应该提前多久预订？

For high-speed trains (TGV, ICE, AVE), book 2-3 months ahead for the best prices. Regional trains usually don't require advance booking. Eurail passes can be purchased anytime before travel.

什么是Eurail通票？

A Eurail Pass is a multi-country train pass valid in 33 European countries. Options include Global Pass (all countries), Select Pass (2-4 countries), and One Country Pass. Best for travelers visiting 3+ countries.

如何预订座位？

High-speed and international trains require seat reservations (€10-30). Regional trains typically don't require reservations. Reservations can be made online or at stations.

退票政策是什么？

Flexible fares: full refund before departure. Semi-flexible: partial refund with fee. Non-flexible (Super Economy, PREM'S): no refund. Travel insurance recommended.

儿童票怎么买？

Children under 4 travel free (no seat). Ages 4-11 typically get 50% off. Family discounts available in Germany, Switzerland, and France. Check specific operator policies.

行李规定是什么？

Most European trains allow 2 large suitcases + 1 carry-on per person. No weight limits, but must fit in luggage racks. Oversized items may require special booking.

列车晚点有赔偿吗？

EU regulation EC 261/2007: 50% refund for delays 60-119 minutes, 100% for 120+ minutes. Submit claims through the operating company's website within 3 months.

如何取票？

Options: mobile tickets (QR code), print-at-home, station pickup from machines or counters. Mobile tickets are most convenient and eco-friendly.

支持哪些支付方式？

Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay. Some operators accept local methods (Sofort in Germany, iDEAL in Netherlands).
