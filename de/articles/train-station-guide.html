4. Safety Tips

Safety Checklist
    Watch your belongings; stations are high-risk areas for pickpockets Avoid lingering alone at stations late at night Use official taxis or Uber Keep valuables in a front-facing bag In emergencies, dial 112 (EU-wide emergency number)
