2. App-Bewertungen nach Land

SNCF Connect (France)
    Vorteile: Beautiful interface, supports English, strong early bird ticket alerts Nachteile: Only covers France, weak cross-border features Besondere Funktionen: PREM'S ticket countdown, price trend charts Download: Search "SNCF Connect" on iOS/Android
DB Navigator (Germany)
    Vorteile: Most comprehensive features, supports English, multi-modal transport (train + bus + subway) Nachteile: Complex interface, steep learning curve Besondere Funktionen: Real-time delay info accurate to the minute, offline timetables Download: Search "DB Navigator" on iOS/Android
Trenitalia (Italy)
    Vorteile: Best loyalty program; CartaFRECCIA points can be redeemed for free tickets Nachteile: No English support, outdated interface Besondere Funktionen: Integrated search for both italo and Trenitalia Download: Search "Trenitalia" on iOS/Android
SBB Mobile (Switzerland)
    Vorteile: Best Swiss transport integration (train + bus + boat + mountain railways) Nachteile: Only covers Switzerland, no English support Besondere Funktionen: Swiss Travel Pass digital management, real-time mountain weather Download: Search "SBB Mobile" on iOS/Android
Renfe (Spain)
    Vorteile: Real-time AVE security queue info, station facility navigation Nachteile: No English support, only covers Spain Besondere Funktionen: +Renfe membership management, Cercanías suburban rail Download: Search "Renfe" on iOS/Android

3. Cross-border Booking Apps

Trainline
    Coverage: 15+ European countries Vorteile: One-stop cross-border booking, user-friendly interface, supports English Nachteile: Charges 5-15% service fee Best for: Multi-country trips, travelers unfamiliar with local languages
Omio
    Coverage: Europe + bus + flight Vorteile: Multi-modal transport comparison, transparent pricing Nachteile: Charges service fees, train info less accurate than specialist apps Best for: Comprehensive travel planning

4. Usage Recommendations

App Selection Checklist
    Single-country deep travel → Use local railway app (most comprehensive features) Multi-country trips → Trainline or Omio (one-stop solution) Seeking lowest price → Local apps (no service fees) Need English → SNCF Connect, DB Navigator, Trainline Loyalty points → Local apps (points are not cross-platform)
Best Combinations Recommended combo: Local railway app (tickets + points) + Trainline (cross-border search) + Google Maps (station navigation)
Budget combo: Local apps only (avoid service fees)
