2. 국별 앱 리뷰

SNCF Connect (프랑스)

  • 장점: Beautiful interface, supports English, strong early bird ticket alerts
  • 단점: Only covers 프랑스, weak cross-border features
  • 특별 기능: PREM'S ticket countdown, price trend charts
  • Download: Search "SNCF Connect" on iOS/Android

DB Navigator (독일)

  • 장점: Most comprehensive features, supports English, multi-modal transport (train + bus + subway)
  • 단점: Complex interface, steep learning curve
  • 특별 기능: Real-time delay info accurate to the minute, offline timetables
  • Download: Search "DB Navigator" on iOS/Android

Trenitalia (이탈리아)

  • 장점: Best loyalty program; CartaFRECCIA points can be redeemed for free tickets
  • 단점: No English support, outdated interface
  • 특별 기능: Integrated search for both italo and Trenitalia
  • Download: Search "Trenitalia" on iOS/Android

SBB Mobile (Switzerland)

  • 장점: Best Swiss transport integration (train + bus + boat + mountain railways)
  • 단점: Only covers Switzerland, no English support
  • 특별 기능: Swiss Travel Pass digital management, real-time mountain weather
  • Download: Search "SBB Mobile" on iOS/Android

Renfe (스페인)

  • 장점: Real-time AVE security queue info, station facility navigation
  • 단점: No English support, only covers 스페인
  • 특별 기능: +Renfe membership management, Cercanías suburban rail
  • Download: Search "Renfe" on iOS/Android

3. Cross-border Booking Apps

Trainline

  • Coverage: 15+ European countries
  • 장점: One-stop cross-border booking, user-friendly interface, supports English
  • 단점: Charges 5-15% service fee
  • Best for: Multi-country trips, travelers unfamiliar with local languages

Omio

  • Coverage: Europe + bus + flight
  • 장점: Multi-modal transport comparison, transparent pricing
  • 단점: Charges service fees, train info less accurate than specialist apps
  • Best for: Comprehensive travel planning

4. Usage Recommendations

App Selection Checklist

  • Single-country deep travel → Use local railway app (most comprehensive features)
  • Multi-country trips → Trainline or Omio (one-stop solution)
  • Seeking lowest price → Local apps (no service fees)
  • Need English → SNCF Connect, DB Navigator, Trainline
  • Loyalty points → Local apps (points are not cross-platform)

Best Combinations

Recommended combo: Local railway app (tickets + points) + Trainline (cross-border search) + Google Maps (station navigation)
Budget combo: Local apps only (avoid service fees)
Convenience combo: Trainline or Omio (one-stop, beginner-friendly)
