1. ICE Route Network

ICE (Intercity-Express) is the high-speed rail system operated by Deutsche Bahn, Germany's national railway company, and one of Europe's most developed high-speed networks.

4. DB Navigator App Guide

Download & Registration

  • iOS: App Store search "DB Navigator"
  • Android: Google Play search "DB Navigator"
  • Supports English interface (Settings → Language → English)

Key Features

  • Booking: Credit card, PayPal, SEPA transfer
  • Digital tickets: Mobile QR code, no printing needed
  • Real-time info: Delay notifications down to the minute
  • Seat reservation: Visual seat map (see occupied seats)
  • Trip planning: Multi-modal (train + bus + subway)
  • Loyalty: BahnBonus points program

5. ICE Onboard Experience

Carriage Classes

  • Second Class: 2+2 layout, comfortable, ideal for most travelers
  • First Class: 2+1 layout, more spacious, free newspapers and meal service
  • Quiet Zone: No phone calls, ideal for work
  • Family Zone: With children's play area

Onboard Services

  • WiFi: Free throughout, decent speed (~10 Mbps)
  • Power: Socket at every seat
  • Dining: Bordbistro offers hot meals and drinks
  • Luggage: Large luggage racks at carriage connections

Savings Checklist

  • Buy Sparpreis 180 days ahead (refreshes at midnight)
  • Travel Tuesday to Thursday
  • Get BahnCard 25 (if annual spend €240+)
  • Use weekend pass (unlimited nationwide travel on weekends)
  • Subscribe to DB promo emails (quarterly sales)
  • Consider FlixTrain (budget alternative on some routes)
