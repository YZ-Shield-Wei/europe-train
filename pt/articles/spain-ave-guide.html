Spain AVE High-Speed Train Guide

2026 Latest: Route Network, Promo Tickets, +Renfe Membership, Seat Classes

Updated June 2026Reading time: 10 min

Key Takeaways

  • AVE reaches 310 km/h, connecting Spain's major cities
  • Promo tickets from €15, open 2 months ahead
  • +Renfe membership €20/year, 10–20% discount
  • AVE requires security check 30 minutes before departure
  • Madrid Atocha and Barcelona Sants are main hubs

3. Seat Classes

  • Turista (Second Class): 2+2 layout, best value
  • Turista Plus (Second Class+): More spacious, includes meal
  • Preferente (First Class): 2+1 layout, includes meal and magazine
  • Club (Business Class): Individual seats, full service

4. Booking Tips

Savings Checklist

  • Buy Promo tickets 2 months ahead (from €15)
  • Register +Renfe membership (€20/year, 10–20% discount)
  • Travel Tuesday to Thursday
  • Avoid August (Spanish holidays) and public holidays
  • Use Renfe app (exclusive offers)

5. Renfe App Guide

  • iOS/Android: Search "Renfe"
  • Supports English interface
  • Key features: Booking, digital tickets, real-time info, seat selection

Important Tips

AVE requires security check 30 minutes before departure (similar to airports). Carry valid ID (passport or national ID). Stations have X-ray security. Madrid Atocha station features a tropical garden — arrive early to explore.
