Rail Passes

Complete European rail pass guide to find your best option

Eurail Global Pass

Global Pass · 33 Countries

For travelers planning to visit multiple countries. Continuous 15 days to 3 months, or flexible 5-15 days.

  • Unlimited travel in 33 European countries
  • Most high-speed trains included (reservation required)
  • Free or discounted ferries and buses
  • Youth (12-27) 25% discount
  • Children (4-11) travel free
€400
15 days continuous, 2nd class

Eurail One Country

Single Country · Deep Exploration

For focused exploration of one country. 3-8 flexible days.

  • Unlimited travel in one country
  • Best for France, Germany, Italy and other large countries
  • More economical than Global Pass
  • Most regional trains included
  • High-speed trains require extra reservation fee
€200
3 days flexible, 2nd class

Swiss Travel Pass

Swiss Pass · All-Inclusive

Switzerland's most comprehensive pass. Includes trains, buses, boats, and museums.

  • Unlimited travel throughout Switzerland
  • Scenic trains included (reservation required)
  • 500+ museums free
  • Mountain railways 50% discount
  • 3-15 days continuous use
CHF 232
3 days continuous, 2nd class

German Rail Pass

German Pass · Flexible

Unlimited travel within Germany. 3-10 flexible days, non-consecutive.

  • Unlimited travel throughout Germany
  • ICE high-speed trains included
  • No reservation required (recommended)
  • One companion travels free
  • Children under 6 travel free
€194
3 days flexible, 2nd class

How to Choose?

Choose Eurail Global if: You plan to visit 3+ countries with large distances (e.g., Paris → Berlin → Rome → Barcelona).

Choose Eurail One Country if: You want to focus on one country (e.g., 10 days in France, 2 weeks in Italy).

Choose Swiss Travel Pass if: You plan to stay in Switzerland for 3+ days, want scenic trains and boat rides.

Choose German Rail Pass if: You want flexible movement within Germany without fixed itinerary.

Skip the pass if: Your itinerary is fixed and booked early - single advance tickets may be cheaper than passes.
