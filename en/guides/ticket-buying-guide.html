How to Buy European Train Tickets

2026 Complete Guide: Official websites, early bird tickets, price comparison, and money-saving tips

Updated June 2026Reading time: 15 minutes

Key Takeaways

  • Buy early bird tickets 2-3 months in advance for savings up to 70%
  • Use official railway websites for best prices (no service fees)
  • France: SNCF Connect (PREM'S from €10)
  • Germany: DB (Sparpreis from €17.90)
  • Italy: Trenitalia (Super Economy from €9.90)
  • Spain: Renfe (Promo from €15)

2. Early Bird Ticket Strategies

Money-Saving Checklist

  • Book 2-3 months in advance (tickets released 3-6 months early)
  • Travel Tuesday-Thursday (cheapest days)
  • Avoid Friday evening and Sunday afternoon
  • Choose off-peak hours (before 7am, after 9pm)
  • Use private operators (italo in Italy, Ouigo in France)
  • Check for regional train alternatives (slower but cheaper)

4. Rail Pass vs Point-to-Point

When to Choose Rail Pass

Choose Eurail Pass if:
- Visiting 3+ countries
- Taking 3+ long-distance trains
- Want flexibility (no fixed dates)
- Value convenience over lowest price

Choose Point-to-Point if:
- Visiting 1-2 countries
- Can book 2-3 months ahead
- Fixed travel dates
- Want lowest price

5. Booking Tips by Country

France

  • SNCF Connect: Best official site, PREM'S tickets released 3 months early
  • Ouigo: Low-cost TGV (no onboard catering, strict luggage limits)
  • TGVmax: Monthly subscription for frequent travelers (€79/month)

Germany

  • DB Navigator: Best app with real-time info
  • Sparpreis: Non-refundable, specific train only
  • Flexpreis: Flexible, any train same day
  • Quer-durchs-Land-Ticket: €44 for regional trains all day

Italy

  • Trenitalia: State operator, wider network
  • italo: Private operator, newer trains, often cheaper
  • CartaFRECCIA: Loyalty program (free tickets with points)

Switzerland

  • SBB Mobile: Best for Swiss travel (includes buses and boats)
  • Supersaver: Limited availability, up to 50% off
  • Swiss Travel Pass: Best for tourists (unlimited travel)

Spain

  • Renfe: Official site, Promo tickets from €15
  • +Renfe: €20/year membership for 10-20% discount
  • OUIGO: Low-cost AVE (Barcelona-Madrid from €9)
