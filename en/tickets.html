Tickets

European train ticket booking guide and money-saving tips

France SNCF

Book via SNCF Connect website or App. Buy 2-3 months in advance for PREM'S fares from €19.

PREM'S from €19

Germany DB

DB Navigator App booking. Sparpreis offers 50% discount for advance purchase, BahnCard holders get extra 25% off.

Sparpreis 50% off

Italy Trenitalia

Super Economy from €9.9 when booked early. Note: non-refundable, confirm your itinerary before booking.

Super Economy from €9.9

Switzerland SBB

Supersaver up to 70% off for early booking. Consider Halbtax half-fare card (annual fee CHF 185).

Supersaver 70% off

Spain Renfe

Promo fares from €15 when booked early. AVE high-speed trains require 30-minute advance arrival for security check.

Promo from €15

Eurail Pass

Multi-country continuous or flexible pass. Best for travelers visiting 3+ countries, 15 days from €400.

15 days from €400

Money-Saving Tips

  • Book 2-3 months in advance for early bird discounts
  • Choose off-peak times (Tuesday to Thursday)
  • Consider rail company membership cards (BahnCard, Halbtax)
  • Compare single tickets vs. passes based on your itinerary
  • Subscribe to rail company promotional emails
  • Use price alert features for fare drop notifications
