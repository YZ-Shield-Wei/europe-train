1. TGV Network

TGV (Train à Grande Vitesse) is the high-speed train system operated by フランス’s national railway SNCF. Since its launch in 1981, it has become one of Europe’s most mature high-speed networks.

3. 予約のコツ

節約チェックリスト

  • Buy PREM'S tickets 3 months ahead (refresh at midnight)
  • Travel Tuesday–Thursday (30% cheaper than weekends)
  • Avoid July–August and French public holidays
  • Buy Carte Avantage youth card (ages 12–27)
  • Use SNCF Connect app (exclusive discounts)
  • デメリットider Ouigo low-cost TGV on select routes

4. 座席選択

座席タイプ

  • 2等車（Second）： 2+2レイアウト、最もコスパが良い
  • 1等車（Première）： 2+1レイアウト、より広々、食事付き
  • 静かな車両（Zen）： 通話禁止、仕事に最適
  • Family area (Famille): Children’s play facilities

座席選択のコツ

Seat Numbering Rules

窓側席： A、F（奇数列）
通路側： C、D
進行方向向き： 奇数列（1、3、5...）
進行方向逆： 偶数列（2、4、6...）

5. SNCF App Guide

ダウンロードと登録

  • iOS: App Store search "SNCF Connect"
  • Android: Google Play search "SNCF Connect"
  • Supports Chinese interface (Settings → Language)

主要機能

  • Booking: Credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay accepted
  • E-ticket: いいえ printing needed, scan QR code at gate
  • Real-time info: Delay and platform change alerts
  • Trip management: Auto-import to calendar
  • Loyalty: Voyageur points program
