Berlin to Munich by Train

Complete 2026 Guide | From €17.90

The Berlin to Munich route is Germany's busiest high-speed rail corridor, covering 623km in just 4 hours.

主な情報

  • 最速所要時間: 3h 52m (ICE direct)
  • 価格帯: €17.90 - €150+
  • 直通列車: Yes, every hour

Booking Tips

Book Sparpreis fares 2-3 months in advance for prices from €17.90. Use BahnCard 25 for additional 25% savings.
