2026 Latest: Glacier Express, Bernina Express, GoldenPass Line Routes, Fares, Best Seasons

Updated June 2026 Reading time: 12 min

Key Takeaways

  • Glacier Express is the world's slowest express train, 8 hours through the Alps
  • Bernina Express is a UNESCO World Heritage route
  • GoldenPass Line connects Lucerne and Montreux via Interlaken
  • Swiss Travel Pass covers most scenic trains
  • Best season: May–October (summer greenery, autumn colors)

1. Glacier Express

The Glacier Express is known as the "world's slowest express train." Covering approximately 290 km across 291 bridges and 91 tunnels, it is Switzerland's most famous scenic train.

Route Information

Feature Details
Route Zermatt → St. Moritz (or reverse)
Distance Approx. 290 km
所要時間 Approx. 8 hours
Operator Matterhorn Gotthard Bahn / Rhaetian Railway
Fare CHF 152 (second class), reservation fee CHF 44
Highlights 291 bridges, 91 tunnels, Landwasser Viaduct

Best Seats

Seat Selection Tips

Zermatt → St. Moritz: Choose right side (better views)
St. Moritz → Zermatt: Choose left side
Panoramic windows: First class has larger panoramic windows
Book early: Reserve 3 months ahead for peak season

2. Bernina Express

The Bernina Express is a UNESCO World Heritage route, traveling from Chur or St. Moritz in Switzerland across the Alps to Tirano in Italy.

Route Information

Feature Details
Route Chur/St. Moritz → Tirano (Italy)
Distance Approx. 170 km (St. Moritz segment)
所要時間 Approx. 4 hours
Operator Rhaetian Railway
Fare CHF 63 (second class), reservation fee CHF 14
Highlights 196 bridges, 55 tunnels, Brusio Spiral Bridge

Best Seats

Seat Selection Tips

St. Moritz → Tirano: Choose right side
Tirano → St. Moritz: Choose left side
Panoramic car: First class has larger windows
Open-air car: Open in summer, best for photography

3. GoldenPass Line

The GoldenPass Line connects Lucerne and Montreux via Interlaken, one of Switzerland's most classic scenic routes.

Route Information

Feature Details
Route Lucerne → Interlaken → Montreux
Distance Approx. 200 km
所要時間 Approx. 5 hours
Operator Swiss Federal Railways / BLS / MOB
Fare CHF 70 (second class), Swiss Travel Pass included
Highlights Lake Brienz, Jungfrau, vineyard terraces

4. Other Scenic Trains

Gotthard Panorama Express

  • Route: Lucerne → Locarno (or reverse)
  • 所要時間: Approx. 5 hours
  • Highlights: Gotthard Tunnel, Lake Lugano
  • Fare: CHF 50 (second class)

Cogwheel Mountain Railways

  • Jungfrau Railway: Europe's highest railway station (3,454 m)
  • Gornergrat Railway: Iconic Swiss peak
  • Pilatus Railway: World's steepest cogwheel railway (48% gradient)

7. Booking Tips

Booking Checklist

  • Book 3 months ahead (especially for summer)
  • Choose panoramic car (first class)
  • Reserve window seat (right or left depending on direction)
  • Consider Swiss Travel Pass (better value)
  • Book meals (available on some trains)
  • Bring a jacket (air conditioning is cold)
  • Prepare your camera (scenery is stunning)

Important Tips

Swiss scenic trains are extremely popular — book 3 months ahead for summer (July–August). Some alpine routes may close in winter due to weather; confirm before traveling. Swiss Travel Pass is the best choice for Swiss scenic trains, covering not only trains but also buses, boats, and museums.
