1. EU規則EC 261/2004

EU Regulation EC 261/2004 defines rail passenger rights, including delay compensation, refunds, and assistance.

適用範囲

  • ✅ Applies to all EU member states
  • ✅ 国際列車に適用 (departing from or arriving in the EU)
  • ✅ 全鉄道会社に適用 (state-owned and private)
  • ❌ 地下鉄、路面電車、都市バスには適用されない

基本権利

旅客の権利

情報を受ける権利: Railway companies must provide timely delay information
支援を受ける権利: Meals and accommodation for delays over 60 minutes
補償を受ける権利: 補償 for delays over 60 minutes
払い戻しを受ける権利: Full refund for delays over 120 minutes

3. How to Apply for Compensation

Required Documents

  • 原本切符（電子または紙）
  • 遅延証明書 (issued by station or APP screenshot)
  • 身分証明書 (passport or ID card)
  • 銀行口座情報 (for refund)

Application Steps

一般的な手順

ステップ1: Record delay duration and reason
ステップ2: Obtain delay certificate (station service center or APP)
ステップ3: Log in to railway company website or APP
ステップ4: Fill out compensation claim form
ステップ5: Upload ticket and delay certificate
ステップ6: Wait for review (usually within 30 days)

4. Refund Rules

Full Refund Conditions

  • 列車運休 (with no alternative provided)
  • Delay over 120 minutes (in some countries)
  • Missing last connecting train
  • Railway company strike (with advance notice)

Partial Refund Conditions

  • Delay over 60 minutes (25% refund)
  • 座席クラスダウングレード (refund of price difference)
  • サービス未提供 (e.g., meals, WiFi)

5. Rebooking Policy

無料 Rebooking

  • Train delay over 60 minutes
  • 列車運休
  • Missed connection due to railway company fault

Paid Rebooking

  • Personal reason changes (usually require a fee)
  • Flexible tickets (Flexpreis/Base) usually allow free changes
  • Saver tickets (Sparpreis/Super Economy) usually non-changeable

6. Special Circumstances

Force Majeure

The following situations usually do not qualify for compensation:

  • 極端な天候 (blizzards, floods)
  • 自然災害 (earthquakes, landslides)
  • 第三者原因 (trespassing, vandalism)
  • 医療緊急事態

鉄道会社 Strikes

ストライキ補償

If the railway company notifies of a strike in advance (usually 48 hours), compensation is typically not provided. However, if no advance notice is given or the strike causes delays over 60 minutes, you may still be eligible for compensation.

7. Practical Tips

遅延時チェックリスト

  • Immediately record delay duration (photo or screenshot)
  • Obtain delay certificate (station service center)
  • Save all tickets and receipts
  • Understand railway company compensation policy
  • Submit compensation claim within 30 days
  • Keep all communication records
  • デメリットider travel insurance (additional coverage)
